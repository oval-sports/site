## Oval Sports Pvt. Ltd.

Oval Sports is a Kathmandu-based sports equipment company bringing quality cricket gear to every player in Nepal — from beginners to professionals.

## Cricsal — Our Cricket Brand

**Cricsal** is the flagship brand under Oval Sports, purpose-built for cricket. Every Cricsal product is tested by professional cricketers, built to international quality standards, and priced to be accessible to every player.

## Our Mission

To ensure that every cricketer in Nepal — regardless of location or budget — has access to professional-grade equipment. We currently supply to retailers across 20+ districts of Nepal and are expanding rapidly.
